Colors

Our Brand palette is comprised of blues to bring boldness to our brand and is used in logical ways throughout our design and marketing to guide the eye and highlight the important bits.

Note: Note that colors on this page are primarily used as a reference for designers and not all are implemented.

60-30-10 Rule for UI Design
60%

The 60% is usually the neutral or primary color. This portion will be used as the base color of the design.

30%

The 30% is the secondary color. It is still visible and will be used for some medium components such as card, carousel, etc.

30%

The 10% is the accent color. This portion will be used for highlight of the design. For example, CTA button, pop up, and some important points of your interface.

Best Practices

Avoid using the same color to mean different things. Use color consistently throughout your interface, especially when you use it to help communicate information like status or interactivity, call of action buttons and so on.
