What is a design system?

A Design System is a set of standards to manage design at scale by reducing redundacy while creating a shared language and visual consistency across-platform that share code, design and interaction behavior.

This design system will guide the teams ensuring UX and UI design consistency for current and future products and as the product’ scales with new features and functions.

While some workflows and designs elements may vary depending on the product’s style guide, we can still use this guide as a starting point. The style guide should also be carry to our promotional materials for brand consistency.
